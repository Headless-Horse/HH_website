HEADLESS HORSE: an Edinburgh-based, full-service design studio working at the intersection of art, design and technology. From growing enterprises to global powerhouses, Horsemen have been called upon to deliver strategy, brand guidelines, interior and digital experiences for brands and progressives, such as Nike, Rolls-Royce and Vogue.

CLIENTELE: Adobe, American Airlines, Coveteur, Forbes, Fortune, GQ, Harley-Davidson, Jordan Brand, Kiehl’s, LYMA, Maxim, Men’s Health, MLB, NBA, Nespresso, Nike, OPI, Rolls-Royce, Sony Pictures, Triumph Motorcycles, Vogue, Yves Saint Laurent, 20th Century Fox.

EXPERTISE: Art direction, branding, consulting, content creation, copywriting, creative development, curation, digital, event design, fabrication, front-end development, graphic design, identity, illustration, interior design, naming, motion, mural design, packaging, photography, sculpture, social content, strategy.

PRESS: Hypebeast, Highsnobiety, FWA, Communication Arts, The Guardian, Vogue, Coveteur, Forbes.

AWARDS: AWWWARDS, CSS Design Awards, Scottish Design Awards.
