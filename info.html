Headless Horse is an independent branding, design, and development studio. Articulating visions through carefully crafted design and rigorous strategic thinking. We take a comprehensive approach to research, design, and development from a project's conception to implementation.
Horsemen have been called upon for strategy, packaging, websites, and artistic direction for the industries of fashion, beauty, culture, and commerce.
According to folklore, blacksmiths would hang horseshoes upside-down over the forge from horses that had returned from battle. The belief was that this would cause luck to pour out, imbuing their work with good fortune.
